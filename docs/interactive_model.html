🚀 Core Upsides & AI Integration

  • Ads Efficiency: AI (Advantage+) matches high-intent users, increasing click-through rates (CTR) and boosting average ad pricing (CPM).
  • Llama Open Source Standard: Commoditizes competitor API structures. Eliminates reliance on OpenAI/Microsoft platforms, preserving Meta's high-margin independence.
  • WhatsApp Messaging: Large-scale business customer service automation powered by agentic AI bots converts a messaging network into a high-utility B2B revenue center.
  • Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses: Consumer ambient AI computing interfaces drive augmented reality adoption.

⚠️ Core Risks & CapEx Overhead

  • Massive Depreciation Drag: A $125B - $145B CapEx ramp creates massive depreciation charges, depressing operating margin if revenue growth tapers.
  • Negative Free Cash Flow: Heavy upfront investment threatens cash generation, restricting buybacks and dividends.
  • Reality Labs Losses: The segment continues to burn ~$18B - $20B per year with delayed commercial AR glasses integration.
  • Ad Market Vulnerability: Advertising spending is highly macro-dependent and faces intense competition from Google, TikTok, and Amazon.
